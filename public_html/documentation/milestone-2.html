Content Strategy:

Welcome:

  • The content inside the welcome content will be a quick 2 to 3 sentence introduction.
  • The introduction will include name, occupation and a background image of Marissa's esthetician set up.

About Marissa:

  • The content inside the "About Marissa" section will detail her professional history, skill set and highlights.
  • This section will also showcase personality and strength she has.
  • Detailed journey on how she chose to be an esthetician.
  • Talks about her passion for helping people with beauty needs.

Services:

  • The content inside the "Service" section will showcase images of her best work.
  • Under the images will be text content on how the service is done and why its done.

Contact Me:

  • Inside the "Contact Me" section will be a text that will tell the user if they like what they see to contact her.
  • Under the text, will be links to a reliable email, phone number, and a link to her social Instagram page.

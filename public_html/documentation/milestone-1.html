Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose:

  • This project will be a showcase portfolio with a focus on front-end web development.
    Using front-end web development will be a necessary skill-set to help accomplish
    making a unique website.

Audience:

  • This site will be designed with a customer audience in mind.The site will target individuals
    in the beauty and skincare community.The target audience would include:beauty enthusiasts,
    individuals that need skincare/beauty,employers.Individuals that are in need of beauty needs
    can visit the site.

Goal:

  • The primary goal of this site is for any user that visits can contact my sister in regards to
    beauty and skincare services.This site will also be used to find employment opportunities.
    Another goal is to showcase services with pictures and what each service provides.
    Each service will have pricing information and a description.

Persona

Name:Sara Otero

Age:24

Profession:

  • Sara just graduated from college and is a full time employee for the City of Albuquerque.
    She is an hourly employee that likes to put in overtime when she can fit it in her schedule.
    Sarah recently got a pay raise for her experience on the job.

Technology:

  • Sara is an Apple power user.She uses a Macbook pro only at home.She also owns an Iphone
    13 pro max with an unlimited 5g data plan where she uses everywhere she goes.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Sara is an over achiever at her workplace which causes her to spend most her time in the
    office.Sara is overly addicted to social media and will spend any free time she has scrolling
    through social media.Sarah is a visual person in which she does not like reading long text.
    She enjoys watching anything beauty related on social media,which includes skincare.
    Sara is very social and friendly.

Frustrations and Needs:

  • Sara is in need to find a trustworthy esthetician where she can go for her beauty and skincare
    needs.Sara is an over achiever, in return she noticed her stress levels shoot up which causes her
    skin to breakout.She has tried other estheticians in the past and cant connect with them on a personal
    level, which bums her out.Sara is looking for someone that can provide a professional service that is
    reliable.Sara wants an esthetician she can connect to on a personal level without feeling uncomfortable.

Goals:

  • Sara is concerned about one thing for her search on her new esthetician:Will this service be done
    professionally? Can sara rely on this person to be trustworthy? Will this person put in the effort
    to connect with me on a personal level? Is this person available or always booked? Sara is a very
    busy person with at her employer. She needs to visually see examples of the services provided.

User Story:

  • As a user, I need to determine whether this person provides a professional service and that is
    available and trustworthy for my beauty/skincare needs.
